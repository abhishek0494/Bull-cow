Instructions!

The Cpu is the master in the game. The Cpu has a 3-digit secret number. The digits must be all different. Then, in turn, the players try to guess the secret number. If the matching digits are in their right positions, they are "bulls", if in different positions, they are "cows". For ex:-if secret number is 413 and your guess is 153 then it is 1 bull and 1 cow. if guess is 356 then 1 cows if guess is 713 then 2 bulls. if you guess 413 you won.
